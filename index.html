Our Services

The RDAB is dedicated to fostering the growth of small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) and strengthening civil society by implementing programs that drive both economic and social development across the region. We provide expert guidance to individuals and businesses in preparing comprehensive business plans, grant proposals, and loan applications, ensuring they are well-equipped to secure funding and achieve sustainable growth. For NGOs, we offer tailored consultancy services in project development, grant applications, and fundraising strategies. Our team also designs and delivers customized training programs for individuals, businesses, and organizations, aligned with their specific needs, while conducting in-depth market research to support informed decision-making. In addition, we organize conferences, seminars, and roundtable discussions on behalf of clients, develop strategic business solutions including marketing plans and bookkeeping systems, and provide thorough monitoring and evaluation of projects to guarantee effective implementation and measurable impact.
